LM coverall is made from LIGHT weight yet MAXIMUMSHIELD material from South Korea and Japan. So, it is called LM COVERALL.

It is a micro porous film laminate that offers barrier protection and breathability at the same level as DuPont's Nexgen and BBA's Linopore products. It also offers superior protection from Bio as well as blood penetration. It is soft, yet strong and durable and is the best alternative to the expensive Tyvek coveralls. We offer our coveralls at 1/3 to 1/2 the price you are currently paying for Tyvek coverall.

Specification: White, 55g, 60g, without elastic at waist, with/without hood, with elastic at wrists and ankles, one-piece torso, V-shape crotch, with 1.5cm reinforcing strips at crotch.

Size: S to 5XL

Now, LM COVERALL has quickly become one of our best sellers in North/South America and Europe.

What is "WECR"?

WECR has a higher performance rating to WEC. All of the stress points are sewn twice and reinforced. That is the reason for the "R".

WECR is used in those environments where protection at a minimum must last 5 hours or more without failure at the critical separation points.

Whether you choose WEC style or WECR style coveralls or products you can be assured that your comfort in movement and utilization will meet or exceed your current demands.

LM MaximumShield Material

LM MaximumShield material is equivalent to DuPont's NexGen, Kimberly Clark's KleenGuard XP and BBA's Linopore.

This material offers breathability and superior protection at a substantially lower cost point than that of the major manufacturers and represents an advance in material development.

LM MaximumShield is made from two materials "married" together. The first material is a specially designed polypropylene textile that makes the material strong and hard to "punch through". The second material is a film that has very small holes in it. (These holes will not permit chemicals to pass through but will permit oxygen to pass through and that is why it is "Breathable").

Also since the film is Polyethylene it means that the outside of the suit is resistant to most chemicals and will not "wet out". The interior material will transport "sweat" away from the wearer making it unique from the other products where moisture "pools" and drips.

What this means to the end user is a product that is reliable, breathable and functional with all of the cost benefits.
