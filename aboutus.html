About Us

Leemei Associates was established in 1988 and has been serving the industry for many years.

Leemei Associates Inc. changed its name to Leemei US LLC in 2005.

In 1995, Leemei invested two manufacturing facilities in China to provide our clients with the best quality products.

We are a leading manufacturer and exporter. Our factories are located in mainland, China. We own the equipments, facilities and control the entire manufacturing process. We provide our customers with the best quality products at most competitive pricing. No middlemen, No agents.

We utilize the most advanced equipments in the world and directly source most of the raw materials from Japan and South Korea. Our Non-woven factory has been awarded Gold Supplier for 10 consecutive years since 2002. All our products are manufactured in ISO 9001:2000 facility and are made to the highest certification standards.

Our products are offered exclusively for wholesalers and distributors in all over the world. We also provide disposable garments with customized label and logo to meet our customer's needs.

Our long-standing commitment to provide total customer satisfaction and quality products has earned us a very solid reputation throughout the industry.

Susan Li

Susan Li is the Owner of Leemei US LLC, a leading manufacturer in disposable products for industry and medical applications.

Li, a China native, earned her master's degree in Business Management at Pace University, New York. She started her career as a senior consultant at Bush International Investment Company in New York. From 1988 to 1995, she served as a Senior Vice President of Leemei Associates Inc. in New York. In 1995, she set up her own company Leemei US and invested Leemei 100% ownership Non-woven manufacturing facilities in China to pursue a business with more opportunities and cost efficiency.

During this period, she became known to many Non-woven buyers and distributors desiring to expand the market for Leemei's products in the States and European countries, for her international trade acumen and expertise. Also, as a result of becoming known for promoting importing/exporting activity for U.S. companies, she was appointed as a senior China Business Consultant for Bush Investment Company.

Li recognized the innovative and groundbreaking technologies that those Non-Woven companies possessed and assisted them in the cultivation of their markets domestically and internationally.

Today, Li is actively involved in leading the expansion of Leemei both domestically and internationally.

The Company's unique LM Coverall and lab coat have proven to be a valuable best seller in the market worldwide. Leemei US moved from New York to New Jersey in 2005.
